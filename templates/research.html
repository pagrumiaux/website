Research

I am currently a PhD student in audio and artificial intelligence, in a collaboration between Orange Labs and GIPSA-lab. I am in the audio research team within Orange Labs, and in the CRISSP team at GIPSA-lab.

My PhD research focuses on the problem of audio source localization and tracking, specifically on speech signals. I am investigating the possibilities of using deep learning on 3D audio signals to improve the existing models.

My other research interests include music information retrieval and music generation.
